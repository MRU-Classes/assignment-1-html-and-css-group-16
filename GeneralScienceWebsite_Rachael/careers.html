Career Options

A General Science degree offers an interdisciplinary and flexible education in biology, chemistry, physics, geology, geography and mathematics. Different from specialized programs, the General Science degree provides a tailor-made curriculum that covers multiple scientific fields, building a broad and adaptable skill set.
