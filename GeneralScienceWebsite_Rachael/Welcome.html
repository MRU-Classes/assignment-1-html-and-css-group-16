About the Program

General Science is a flexible and interdisciplinary program that equips students with knowledge across biology, chemistry, physics, and more. This post-secondary program encompasses the study of the natural world, involving systematic observation, experimentation, and analysis. Students majoring in General Sciences aim to gain knowledge regarding the universe and its components, covering various aspects such as all living beings and physical sciences within our planet we reside in, even the universe that lays beyond. Courses available cover fields like biology, chemistry, physics, and astronomy. The General Science degree provides students partaking in this program a foundation for understanding how things work in the world around us, with applications ranging from everyday activities to complex technologies, laying a path to many scientific careers in the future.
